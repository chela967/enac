Who We Are

A Catalyst for Agro-Nutrition in the Elgon Region

Elgon Nutri Agro Consultancy (ENAC) was established in 2025 to address the pressing challenges of food insecurity, malnutrition, and climate risks facing communities in Uganda's Elgon sub-region.

Headquartered in Central Division, Kapchorwa Municipality, we operate across Kapchorwa, Kween, Bukwo, and Bulambuli districts — delivering evidence-based solutions that strengthen food and nutrition security for lasting community resilience.

What We Do

Our Core Services

From on-the-ground training to policy advocacy, ENAC delivers a comprehensive suite of agro-nutrition services designed to build lasting resilience across the Elgon sub-region.

🌾

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Equipping farmers with practical knowledge and tools to adapt to changing climate conditions while improving productivity and protecting natural resources.

🥗

Nutrition-Sensitive Farming

Promoting dietary diversity through nutrition-sensitive agricultural practices that improve household food security and health outcomes for communities.

📊

Research & Knowledge Sharing

Generating and disseminating evidence-based knowledge through our planned Agro-Nutrition Knowledge Hub and strategic partnerships with research institutions.

🏪

Value Addition & Market Linkages

Connecting smallholder farmers to profitable markets and supporting value addition processes to increase farm incomes across the Elgon value chain.

Engaging government, development partners, and private sector actors to build strategic partnerships and influence policies that support agro-nutrition security.

Deep Dive

Our Focus Areas

Climate-Smart Agriculture

ENAC builds farmer capacity to adopt technologies and practices that simultaneously boost productivity, enhance resilience to climate variability, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions across the Elgon region.

  • Drought-tolerant crop varieties and seed systems
  • Conservation agriculture and soil health management
  • Efficient irrigation and water harvesting techniques
  • Agroforestry integration for carbon sequestration
  • Early warning systems and climate information services

Community Empowerment

ENAC places communities at the center of all interventions, ensuring that women, young people, and vulnerable households are active participants in shaping solutions for their food and nutrition security.

  • Women-led community savings & credit groups
  • Youth agribusiness incubation programs
  • Nutrition education for mothers and caregivers
  • Community health and nutrition garden projects
  • Inclusive governance and decision-making processes

Market Access & Value Addition

ENAC bridges the gap between smallholder farmers and profitable markets through digital platforms, infrastructure support, and value chain development that ensures farmers receive fair prices for their produce.

  • Digital market information and price discovery systems
  • Collective marketing through farmer cooperatives
  • Post-harvest handling and storage solutions
  • Food processing and value-addition enterprises
  • Linkages with agro-dealers, exporters, and processors

Research & Innovation

ENAC drives evidence-based transformation through our commitment to rigorous research, knowledge generation, and the adoption of proven innovations that improve outcomes for farmers and communities across the Elgon region.

  • Field trials and demonstration plots for new technologies
  • Household dietary diversity and nutrition assessments
  • Agro-Nutrition Knowledge Hub (launching 2027)
  • Digital platforms for e-learning and market linkages
  • Partnerships with universities and research institutions
